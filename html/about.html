ABOUT ME

SOFTWARE ENGINEER & AI ENTHUSIAST

Hello, I'm Susana. I'm currently pursuing Informatics Engineering at ISEP, where I'm on the fast track to becoming an AI engineer, though I absolutely love software engineering too.

I'm a proud girly girl with a passion for clean, aesthetic design. This shows in my work, especially in front-end development, which I genuinely enjoy. My portfolio's style—elegant, minimalist, with a touch of sophistication—draws inspiration from Vogue magazine and reflects my personal aesthetic.

The intersection of technical precision and creative expression is where I thrive. Whether I'm diving into AI algorithms or crafting beautiful user interfaces, I bring both analytical thinking and creative flair to everything I create.

My goal is to blend cutting-edge technology with visually stunning design, creating digital experiences that are not only functional but also aesthetically pleasing. I believe the best technology should be as beautiful as it is powerful.
